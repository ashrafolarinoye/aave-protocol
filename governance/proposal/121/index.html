Proposal overview

Risk Parameter Updates for Aave V2 ETH Market (2022-11-22)

Executed

Executed on 

Nov 27, 2022

Summary

A proposal to make parameter changes on Aave V2 ETH. Given that the market situation of these assets is currently volatile, out of an abundance of caution, we recommend temporarily freezing the following markets:

  • YFI
  • CRV
  • ZRX
  • MANA
  • 1INCH
  • BAT
  • sUSD
  • ENJ
  • GUSD
  • AMPL
  • RAI
  • USDP
  • LUSD
  • xSUSHI
  • DPI
  • renFIL
  • MKR

Specification

Freeze the YFI, CRV, ZRX, MANA, 1inch, BAT, sUSD, ENJ, GUSD, AMPL, RAI, USDP, LUSD, xSUSHI, DPI, renFIL, and MKR markets on Aave V2 ETH.

Implementation

We freeze the YFI, CRV, ZRX, MANA, 1inch, BAT, sUSD, ENJ, GUSD, AMPL, RAI, USDP, LUSD, xSUSHI, DPI, renFIL, and MKR markets by calling the freezeReserve method on the LendingPoolConfigurator
